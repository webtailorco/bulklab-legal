Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

BulkLab ("we", "our", "us") is operated by WebTailor LLC. This policy explains how we handle your data.

What Data We Collect

BulkLab stores your data locally on your device. The following information stays on your phone and is not uploaded to our servers unless you use AI features:

  • Profile information (name, age, gender, height, weight)
  • Nutrition logs (food entries, calories, macronutrients)
  • Workout logs (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Body measurements and progress photos
  • Weight history and body composition estimates
  • Water and supplement tracking
  • Streaks and achievement data

AI Coach Features

When you use the AI Coach, your coaching messages and relevant tracking data (calories, workouts, weight trends) are sent to our servers to generate personalized coaching responses. This data is processed by Anthropic's Claude AI and is not stored permanently on their servers. We store coaching conversations to improve the service.

AI Food Logging

When you use the AI food logging feature, text descriptions and/or photos of food are sent to our servers for nutritional analysis. Photos are processed and not permanently stored.

Apple Health

If you grant permission, BulkLab can read step count data from Apple Health. This data stays on your device and is used only to display your daily activity. We do not upload Apple Health data to any server.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed through Apple's App Store. We use RevenueCat to manage subscription status. We do not have access to your payment information — Apple handles all billing.

Data Storage

Your fitness data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted database. We do not have access to your local data. If you delete the app, all local data is permanently deleted.

Third-Party Services

  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — Powers the AI coach and food analysis features
  • RevenueCat — Manages subscription status
  • Supabase — Hosts our backend services
  • USDA FoodData Central & Open Food Facts — Food nutrition databases

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is shared only with the service providers listed above, solely to provide app functionality.

Children's Privacy

BulkLab is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You can delete all your data at any time by deleting the app. All local data is permanently removed. To request deletion of any server-side data (coaching conversations), contact us at support@webtailorco.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
